Quick decision guide
Count connected regions / components?→Pattern 1 — BFS/DFS + visited
Min steps, unit weights?→Pattern 2 — BFS
Min cost path, non-negative weights?→Pattern 3 — Dijkstra + min-heap
Dependencies / ordering / cycle detection?→Pattern 4 — topological sort
Dynamic merging / connectivity queries?→Pattern 5 — Union-Find DSU
Split into two non-conflicting groups?→Pattern 6 — bipartite 2-coloring
